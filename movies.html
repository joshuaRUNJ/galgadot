MOVIES

Some of her most popular movies include Wonder Woman (2017), Fast Five (2011), Keeping Up with the Joneses (2016), Ralph Breaks the Internet (2018), Death on the Nile (2022), Red Notice (2021), and Knight and Day (2010). She has also appeared in other movies such as Justice League (2017), Batman v Superman: Dawn of Justice (2016), and Furious 7 (2015). Gal Gadot has achieved global stardom for her portrayal of Wonder Woman in the DC Extended Universe. She has won several awards and nominations for her performances in movies and TV shows. She is also a motorcycle enthusiast and owns a black 2006 Ducati Monster-S2R.
